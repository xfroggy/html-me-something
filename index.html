Tao Expeditions April 2019 - Coron to El Nido, 4 nights/5days

In April of 2019, My husband and I took one of the best trips of our lives, to Palawan in the Phillipines. Part of the trip consisted of a 5 day island hopping excursion from Coron to El Nido, Palawan.

Map

With our crew of local islanders, our excursion of a lifetime started in Coron and took us through remote islands, deserted beaches, and hidden coves of Norther Palawan over 5 days. Each night we slept on a new, deserted island and woke to a fabulous meal cooked by our crew.

Our Ship

We would spend most of the day on the ship, navigating from island to island, stopping every now and then for snorkeling or exploring

Night 1

Our first night's accomodations was at the small village where one of the crew members lives. Our hut let us know that we were definitely going to be roughing it on this excursion. But the surroundings were beautiful and made it all worthwhile.

Night 2

Arriving exactly at sunset, our second night's accomodations proved to be superior. Not a soul other than our group could be found on this island. Jungle juice and dinner right on the beeach.

Night 3

Our third night's accomodation happened at the Tao Expedition training center where they train their chefs and massage therapist. In addition to the beachfront views from our hut, we were treated to a 5 course tasting menu and a massage.

Night 4

After snorkeling and seeing various sea turtles and other fish, we arrived to our final night's accomodation after sunset and couldn't appreciate the beauty until having coffee at sunrise the next morning. Please, never let me leave this place!

Food

Although we thought we would be roughing it on this trip, the food was exceptional. Each meal prepared with new and fresh ingredients with delightful flavours.

Activities

From snrokeling to kayaking, our days were filled with activities but also plenty of relaxation.
